# Business Insights

1. Tech has the highest average revenue among categories.
2. Mobile traffic gives the highest average revenue by device.
3. Us is the strongest country by average revenue.
4. `watch_time_minutes` is the strongest numerical revenue driver after the target itself.
5. Videos with stronger watch time usually create better monetization potential.
6. Higher likes and comments improve engagement quality and can support higher revenue.
7. Subscriber base matters, but subscriber engagement is more useful than raw subscribers alone.
8. Very low video length can limit watch-time opportunity even when views are good.
9. Very long videos still need retention; length alone does not guarantee revenue.
10. Country mix should be tracked because CPM can vary by market.
11. Device mix is useful for planning thumbnails and viewing experience.
12. The model can support creators before publishing by testing expected performance scenarios.
13. Content categories with high average revenue can guide future content calendars.
14. Improving comment activity can lift the interaction score and revenue score.
15. The what-if simulator helps convert model output into practical creator actions.
